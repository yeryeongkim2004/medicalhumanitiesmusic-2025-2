# Miracle in Cell No.7

The film <The Gift of Room 7> is a touching story about a pure-hearted intellectually disabled father, Yong-gu, and his most beloved daughter, Ye-seung. It is also a film that does not end simply with a tragic story of a disabled person, but also accuses the society of prejudice against the disabled and the unfair justice system, and asks back the essence of human dignity and love.

In "The Gift of Room 7," a father with an intellectual disability follows the process of dedicating himself to his daughter, going through social gaze and the injustice of the system, and newly confirming the meaning of his family and his existence. In [사운드 오브 메탈],(jeon-junwoo.md)the main character, who was a band drummer, depicts his previous life faltering after losing his hearing and asking and accepting who he is in the new community. Both films do not portray disability or loss as simply a "defect to overcome" but tell the story that human dignity can be restored through human weakness, changes in relationships caused by it, the gaze of society, and eventually love and acceptance.

영화7번방의 선물’에서는 지적장애를 가진 아버지가 딸을 위해 헌신하고 사회적 시선과 체제의 부당함을 겪으며 가족과 자신의 존재 의미를 새롭게 확인하게 되는 과정을 따라가고, [사운드 오브 메탈] (jeon-junwoo.md)에서는 밴드 드러머였던 주인공이 청각을 상실한 뒤 이전 삶이 흔들리고 새로운 공동체 속에서 자신이 누구인지 다시 묻고 받아들이는 여정을 그리는데, 두 영화 모두 장애나 상실을 단순히 ‘극복해야 할 결함’으로만 그리지 않고 사람이 가진 약함과 그것이 야기하는 관계의 변화, 사회의 시선, 그리고 결국엔 사랑과 수용을 통해 인간으로서의 존엄이 회복될 수 있다는 이야기를 담고 있다.
